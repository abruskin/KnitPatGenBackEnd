Hem/Edge: 
CO 66 stitches, place marker and join in round being careful not to twist.
Round 1: *k1 p1* repeat until end of round 
 Work ribbing as established until measures 1 
Main Body: Knit every round until measures 4.0 inches from CO edge
Decrease section: 
Round 1: *k11.0 pm * repeat across round 
Round 2: *k to 2 before marker, k2tog* repeat across round 
Round 3: k across round 
Repeat rounds 2-3 until 8 stitches remain 
 Cut yarn, thread through remaining live stitches. Draw tight and weave in ends.
